Our Mission And Values



Our Mission


Our mission is to promote "Educational Excellence" and "Balanced Growth" in a challenging, safe, and inclusive learning environment for the benefit of intellectually capable children of a sound character whose talents, interests, and uniqueness will be encouraged and nurtured to prepare them to lead, serve, and become contributing citizens to our country, and the global community.




Our Core Values


From its earliest days, GGPS has emphasized both "Academic excellence" and "Character development".

Today's GGPS values students for their potential as well as their achievement and emphasizes balanced growth.


- Intellectual, Physical, Social, and Ethical.

Gyan Ganga Public School recognizes that the primary responsibility for instilling and strengthening values resides with parents. Nevertheless, a school can and should play an important role in character education.



We have established four core values at the heart of our emphasis on character development.


- Honesty :

Individuals of the GGPS community tell the truth and act with integrity and honor. We do not mislead, cheat, steal, or lie.


- Respect :

Individuals of the GGPS community affirm the intrinsic dignity of all people. We act with tolerance, courtesy, and thoughtful regard for all persons, for property, for the environment, and for ourselves.


- Responsibility :

Individuals of the GGPS community are reliable and hold themselves and other accountable for their actions. We do not make excuses, blame others, or take unwarranted credit.


- Kindness :

Individuals of the GGPS community exhibit caring and compassionate behavior in all aspects of daily life. We are not mean, and we do not harass, nor act in a cruel manner. By our positive example, we discourage unkind behavior in others.

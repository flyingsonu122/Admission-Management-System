Dear Visitors,


Welcome to Gyan Ganga Public School. Here at the GGPS we encourage each of our students to be a self-confident and well-rounded individual. We aim to propel them to the highest level of achievement in both academics and extra curricular activities to provide them with the life skills they will need when they leave the school and to prepare them to be of value to society.


GGPS is a warm community which lives out high standards and is rightly proud of its achievements. I often say our children become renaissance young men and women during their time at GPS. From strong academics to an inclusive faith life, our students explore their creativity and curiosity and become confident young adults.


We are a small enough school for each pupil to be known well, but large enough to offer a wide range of subjects and experience. Students at Gyan Gang Public School discover "Who they are?" through a program of excellence, integrated curriculum, personalized attention, and a faith that guides us to serve others along the way also they are encouraged to be curious and that comes alive through creativity, critical thinking, faith-based character development, and collaboration. This enables our children to become confident who are prepared to lead a life of significance.


It is no coincidence that our graduating students site their service work and caring community as two of the most important aspects of their time at GGPS. This combined with an almost 100% acceptance rate at the high school of their choice signifies the great work of this community.


I take pride in being the principal of such institution like GGPS, Please do come to campus and get an even better understanding of the our welcoming and supportive faculty, administrators and students and see for yourself what Gyan Ganga Public School can offer your son or daughter. I look forward to welcoming you and your family to Gyan Gang Public School. Thank you!


Sincerely,



Soni Kumari

Principal, GGPS

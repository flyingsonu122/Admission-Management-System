Upper School at Gyan Ganga Public School


Grades 5-8 are leaders on campus

Challenging academics in the Upper School at GGPS, in concert with community service, athletics, visual and performing arts, extra curricular activities, and social events, provide excellent preparation for students to enter the higher institution of their choice.


Continued emphasis on leadership skills and character development, with guidance from teachers and advisers, is a hallmark of the Upper School experience and the reason GPS stands out among middle schools in the city.


                    Beginning in fifth grade, students:


                              - Focus on developing good life skills.

                              - Receive instruction on organization, interpretation, retention, test taking, studying, and listening.


Students at Gyan Gang Public School receive a firm educational foundation, become part of a true community, and develop leadership skills and by the time they complete eighth grade, students have demonstrated their maturity and readiness that translate to their success in higher educations and beyond.


Gyan Gang Public School makes it less of a challenge to find a superior upper school. Bhadasi is the home of our upper school which serves students in fifth grade through eighth grade. The positive reputation we hold in academics and life skills is the reason families favor our upper school!
